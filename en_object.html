OBJECTS OF DESIRE

As we enter into the age of technology, a new crop of home products that redefine the meaning of "quality life" emerge.

Lutron Automated Shades and Home Control Systems

Automated shades are a hot item for smart homes. Lutron has always been in the forefront of this development. Its new generation of blinds offer a range of protection options from sheer, dim-out, to blackout fabrics. Whether it is your bedroom windows, skylights, ventilation windows, or windows in hidden corners of your home, with the touch of a button on the remote control, you can easily manage your blinds with ultra-quiet performance and precision. You can choose to have your shades start, move, and stop in unison, maintaining perfect alignment with each other. Or you can have individual blinds at different heights and angles, which can all be programmed for ease of control. The HomeWorks QS series, designed for larger homes, integrates the control of all your light, interior and exterior, electric light and daylight. The system also integrates with audio-visual and HVAC control, all under the control of a remote control, smart phone, or smart watch.

Philips Luminous Textile

Luminous textile has been gaining popularity in the home in the last few years. It integrates multi-coloured LEDs seamlessly within beautiful fabric panels that also soften sound. You can create an accent wall with the colours or images you love. Imagine a wall that can display your favourite image of a sunrise or an endless field of flowers. You can change the image according to your mood, creating the perfect ambient for your home any time of the day.

B&B Italia

Timeless design never goes out of style. B&B Italia is one of the few furniture companies that has achieved this status. Ever since its inception in the 1960’s, B&B Italia has put its focus on research and design. Its internal R&D Center reflects its commitment to produce quality furniture that combines technology, creativity, and inherent style. The company works with internationally renowned architects and designers, such as Antonio Citterio, Patricia Urquiola, and Zaha Hadid, redefining the concept of sofa with the latest technology and unique designs.

Sonos: The Home Sound System

Music brings joy our lives, and the Sonos wireless sound system can deliver that joy to you through your home wifi with a warm full-bodied sound that is crystal clear at any volume. The Sonos system enables you to access to different sources from the most popular music services, internet radio, audiobooks, podcasts and music downloads to play and stream different music in multiple rooms or enjoy one big favourite in perfect sync all through your house. All you need to do is download the free app to your Apple or Android-based smart phone, tablet, or computer and you can enjoy whatever you love to listen to, in any and every room in your home.

Samsung QLED TV features Quantum Dot technology to achieve 100% colour volume, so you can experience rich, perfect colors that stay true. Its ultra-thin screen and Invisible Connection design, which uses invisible optical cable and remove unnecessary clutter, makes it look more like an artwork than a TV. The improved, voice-control OneRemote puts you in control of all your devices with a single remote. Say goodbye to juggling numerous remote controls while trying to watch your favourite show.

B&O BeoVision Avant & BeoLab 18

The state-of-the-art BeoVision Avant TV features Ultra-High Definition (4K) images and local dimming to optimise your viewing experience. Its powerful 3-channel speaker system harnesses 3 dedicated tweeters, 3 midrange units and 2 powerful bass drivers to deliver the highest quality of sound. But why stop there? Connect your BeoVision Avant TV with BeoLab 18 speakers and you will get an even stunning sound performance. Beautifully crafted in a combination of wooden lamellae and aluminum, BeoLab 18 wireless setup requires just a single power cable to each speaker. The Acoustic Lens Technology spreads optimal sound quality evenly through the room with a 180-degree sound performance. Using the WiSA technology with a 5GHz frequency, the sound quality is protected from interference of surrounding networks.

Crestron Smart Home System

An efficient smart home system allows you to control various components of your home with a central operating system. Crestron Smart Home System from the U.S. offers you the ability to remotely control the lighting, a/c units, blinds, and audios-visual and security systems of your home. You can program different settings for lighting and temperature to suit your unique lifestyle. With remote access from your mobile phone, you can create the perfect home setting before you even get home.
